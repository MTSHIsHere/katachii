About the Project

Katachi Onsha is an original Japanese UTAU voicebank developed by SynthDragon Studios. The project focuses on creating a clear, expressive male voice suitable for emotional, pop, and lo-fi music.

This project is independently managed and maintained, including character design, website development, voicebank distribution, and documentation.

Future Plans

  • Re-recording the voicebank with improved recording equipment
  • Enhancing pronunciation consistency and sound quality
  • Expanding documentation and usage examples
  • Exploring future compatibility with additional synthesis platforms

The long-term goal is to refine Katachi Onsha into a high-quality, ethically produced voice synthesis project that meets professional standards.
