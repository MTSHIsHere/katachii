About the Project

Katachi Onsha is an original Japanese UTAU voicebank developed by SynthDragon Studios. The project focuses on a clear, expressive male voice for emotional, pop, and lo-fi music.

The project is independently managed, including character design, website development, distribution, and documentation.

Future Plans

  • Re-recording with improved recording equipment.
  • Better pronunciation consistency and overall sound quality.
  • Expanded documentation and usage examples.
  • Future compatibility with additional synthesis platforms.
